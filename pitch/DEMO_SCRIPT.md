# LUDEX -- Demo Script
### 3-Minute Live Demo
#### OneHack 3.0 | AI & GameFi Edition

---

## Before You Begin

- Backend server running: `cd src/backend && uvicorn server:app --reload --port 8000`
- Landing page open in browser: serve `src/frontend/index.html` (e.g., `python3 -m http.server 3000` from `src/frontend/`)
- App page ready: `src/frontend/app.html`
- Have at least 2-3 players already registered so the leaderboard has data
- Practice the NPC chat at least once -- know what Professor Luna will say about budgeting
- Have a quest available to generate a challenge from

**Tone:** Confident. Conversational. Like showing a friend something incredible you built last night.

---

## [0:00 - 0:20] THE HOOK -- Landing Page

"Let me show you something.

Right now, 200 million young people in Latin America cannot answer a basic question about compound interest. They have never been taught. And the tools that exist? Textbooks. PDFs. Forty-minute lectures.

Nobody finishes those.

But you know what they do finish? Games. Quests. Leaderboards.

This is Ludex."

> *Slowly scroll down the landing page. Show the feature cards, the NPC avatars (owl, bull, wizard, fox), the curriculum section with 6 categories and 23 lessons. Pause briefly on the live leaderboard section. Stop at the registration form.*

## [0:20 - 0:50] THE APP -- Registration & Home

> *Click "Comenzar Aventura" or scroll to the registration form. Fill in a wallet address and username.*

"I am going to register right now. Wallet address, username -- that is it."

> *Submit the form. The app redirects to `app.html`. The Home tab loads showing the player card.*

"This is my dashboard. Level 1, zero XP. I can see my streak counter, my avatar, and six financial categories -- budgeting, investing, saving, credit, crypto, and taxes. Each one has lessons and quests waiting."

> *Point out the player card with level, XP bar, and streak. Scroll down to show the category grid with lesson progress indicators.*

---

## [0:50 - 1:30] THE QUEST -- AI-Generated Challenge

> *Tap the Quests tab in the bottom navigation. Show the list of quests with colored category borders and difficulty stars.*

"Let me take a quest. Here is 'Regla 50/30/20' -- a budgeting challenge."

> *Tap the quest card. The quest detail modal opens showing description, difficulty, XP reward, and token reward.*

"I click 'Generate Challenge' and the AI creates a quiz tailored to my level."

> *Click "Generar Desafio". Wait for the AI to generate questions. The quiz modal appears with a progress bar.*

"Three multiple-choice questions, generated by Claude in real-time. Let me answer them."

> *Read the first question aloud. Select an answer. Click "Siguiente". Repeat for all questions. On the last one, click "Enviar".*

"And... I passed! 150 XP earned, tokens rewarded."

> *Show the results screen with score, XP gained, and confetti animation. Close the modal and show the updated XP bar on the Home tab.*

---

## [1:30 - 2:00] THE AI NPC -- Chat with Professor Luna

> *Tap the NPCs tab. Show the grid of 4 NPC mentors with their SVG avatars.*

"Ludex has four AI mentors, each with a specialty. Professor Luna teaches budgeting. Trader Rex covers investing. Crypto Sage handles DeFi. Credit Fox does debt strategy. All powered by Claude."

> *Tap on Professor Luna (the owl with graduation cap). The full-screen chat opens with suggested prompts.*

"Let me ask Luna about budgeting."

> *Type or tap a suggested prompt: "Como puedo empezar a hacer un presupuesto?" Wait for the typing indicator and the AI response.*

"See that? Luna is not reading from a script. She adapts to my level, explains concepts conversationally, and stays in character. Every conversation is personalized. Every chat earns XP -- capped daily so you cannot farm it."

> *Show the AI response in the chat. Point out the typing indicator and the educational content in the response.*

## Post-Demo Notes

- If judges ask about tokenomics: emphasize soulbound credentials (non-speculative), reward pool funded by in-game economy, and B2B license revenue
- If judges ask about AI safety: Claude's constitutional AI ensures NPC conversations stay educational, age-appropriate, and financially accurate
- If judges ask about blockchain: the Move contract is comprehensive (878 lines) covering all game logic. The backend-to-chain integration follows an oracle pattern where the backend validates achievements before recording them on-chain
- If judges ask about wallet integration: the current prototype uses manual wallet addresses; full Petra/Martian wallet integration is the immediate next step
- If they ask "why LATAM?": because that is where the pain is sharpest, smartphone penetration is high, gaming culture is massive, and nobody is serving this market with anything that works
- If they ask about deployment: the contract targets OneChain testnet; current demo runs the full game loop off-chain with the contract ready for deployment
